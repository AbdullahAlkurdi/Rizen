# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-30

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create a Rizen account, we collect:
- Name or display name
- Email address
- Authentication credentials (managed via Firebase Authentication)

### Usage Data

To provide and improve our services, we process:
- Habit and routine data
- Task completion records and streak information
- Journal entries, notes, and reflections
- AI coaching interactions and feedback
- Domain tracker entries (sports, study, work, coding, cooking/nutrition,
  spiritual, custom)

### Device Information

- Device type, operating system, and version
- Application version
- Unique device identifiers for push notifications and analytics

### Location Information

Location data is collected **only** when necessary to calculate
prayer times and prayer-relative scheduling. This processing is
performed with your explicit consent and is limited to coordinates
required for astronomical calculations.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- **Provide the service:** Operate and maintain Rizen functionality,
  including scheduling, tracking, and AI coaching.
- **Personalize experience:** Adapt routines, recommendations, and
  coaching to individual behavior patterns and preferences.
- **Improve the application:** Analyze aggregated, anonymized usage
  patterns to identify bugs, prioritize features, and enhance
  performance.
- **AI coaching:** Process contextual data through Google Gemini to
  deliver personalized behavioral insights. This processing is
  performed only with your explicit consent for AI features.

## 3. Data Storage

### Primary Storage

User data is stored using **Google Firebase** services:
- **Cloud Firestore:** Operational database for user profiles,
  habits, routines, notes, and domain tracker entries.
- **Firebase Storage:** Media assets and attachments.
- **Firebase Authentication:** Secure credential management.

Firebase infrastructure is GDPR-compliant and operates under Google's
data processing agreements.

### Data Retention

- Account data is retained for the lifetime of your account.
- You may request account deletion at any time, which triggers
  permanent deletion of associated data within 30 days.
- Session tokens and temporary cache data are retained only as long
  as necessary to provide service functionality.

### Data Location

User data is stored in Firebase data centers. Specific geographic
storage locations are determined by Firebase infrastructure and may
vary based on your region and Firebase project configuration.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third
parties for marketing purposes.

### Service Providers

Data may be shared with the following service providers strictly for
the purpose of delivering Rizen functionality:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Handling |
|---|---|---|
| **Firebase** (Google) | Database, auth, storage | Subject to Google's data processing terms |
| **Google Gemini** | AI coaching and parsing | Data is anonymized where possible; subject to Google's AI data policies |

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in
good-faith belief that such action is necessary to:

- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect and defend our rights or property
- Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the service
- Protect the personal safety of users or the public

## 5. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
- **Data Portability:** Request export of your data in a structured,
  machine-readable format.
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for AI processing at any time
  through application settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@rizen.app.

## 6. Cookies

Rizen uses cookies and similar technologies **only** for essential
service functionality:

- **Authentication tokens:** Maintain secure login sessions.
- **Preferences:** Store user interface settings.

We do **not** use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any
third-party analytics cookies without your explicit consent.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Rizen is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we
become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal
information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes
in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

We will notify users of material changes through:
- Application notifications
- Email communication to registered addresses
- Updates to this page with a revised "Last Updated" date

Continued use of Rizen following notice of changes constitutes
acceptance of the updated policy.
